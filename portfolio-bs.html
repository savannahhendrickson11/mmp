Portfolio

"Goldstein National Bank Logo"

This is for the Visual Communications class. I had to take a fellow classmate's surname and design a logo for the client of that name based on the type of brand. For this project, I used Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign.

"I Think There's A Flaw in My Code"

This was the final project for a Digital Imaging class. I used the combination photos and text to create this project using both Adobe Photoshop and In Design.

"Wild Kingdom"

This was a projet for a Multimedia Arts. I was practicing the use of Adobe Photoshop and its tools. With it, I took some picture to make a random collage.

"Photographic Alphabet"

This was a project done for a typography class. I had to take photos of logos with words and put them all together using Adobe Photoshop to make up the alphabet.

"Vintage Photo Recoloring"

This was the first project in the Digital Imaging class. I took an vintage photo of two women walking in the park and recolored it using Photoshop.

Anime GIF: Tears and the Rain

This project was also for the Digital Imaging class. I illustrated the girl crying in the rain in Adobe Illustrator and animated the scene into a GIF using Adobe Photoshop for this project.

Money Woes

This project was for my 2D Animation Class. I illustrated the three characters and the settings in Adobe Illustrator. Then, I animated the scenes and added dialouge text using Adobe Animate.

October 2017, Savannah Hendrickson's Portfolio, Brooklyn, NY, 11236
