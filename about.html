About Me

I am a graphic designer and illustrator. I am currently a student at Borough of Manhattan Community College studying Multimedia Arts. I am studying communication design, typography, web design, and animation. After graduating from BMCC, I would continue my studies in Media Arts in either Pratt Institute, New York City College of Technology, or The New School. Some of my professional goals in the future is to design logos for businesses and characters for comics.
